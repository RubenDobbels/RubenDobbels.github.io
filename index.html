Privacy notice
C & W wants to focus on the protection of the personal data according to the guidelines developed in
the GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation) according to the European Regulation 2016/679 that
applies from 25 May 2018.
We take good care of your personal data

.

We process your personal data in a conscious way
Your personal data will only be processed if this happens in a legal way. On the
one hand, this is possible after your explicit consent. You always have the right to
withdraw this permission.
On the other hand, this is only possible when it is necessary for one of the
following reasons;
execute legal vital common legitimate
contract provisions importance interest interest
Purpose and legality
C & W will only process the necessary personal data in the context of executing an agreement (eg a
purchase), a legitimate interest (eg direct marketing) or according to the legal provisions (eg processing
data own employees).
It goes without saying that C & W securely protects all data. As a result, we have taken the necessary
technical and organizational measures: C & W provides, among other things, the necessary physical
safeguards, regular updates of hardware and software, adequate password management, secure
internet connections and anti-virus software used to achieve this.
The personal data processed by C & W will not be passed on to third parties, unless the transfer is part
of an agreement to be performed (eg direct delivery / service, licenses, subcontracting). This third
party must process the personal data supplied to him in accordance with the GDPR legislation (eg do
not use it for his own purposes, secure this data adequately).
Because C & W keeps you informed, a mailing can be sent to our customers and prospects. We support
customers with the principle of legitimate interest. However, it is possible to unsubscribe in a simple
way at any time so that you no longer receive this mailing
Prospects via the website can indicate whether they want to be mailed, by placing a checkmark on the
contact form on our website. If C & W wants to process your personal data for any other reason, we
will ask for your explicit consent.
